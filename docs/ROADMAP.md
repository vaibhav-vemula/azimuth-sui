# Azimuth — Future Roadmap

---

## Phase 1 — Mainnet Launch
- Deploy OrbitalVault and CreditRegistry to Hedera Mainnet
- Launch AZM token with full reward pool
- Onboard first external ground station operators
- Public station registration open to anyone with LoRa hardware

---

## Phase 2 — Credit Score & Multiplier System
- Implement on-chain credit scoring (PoA epochs + PoRx receptions)
- Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum tier progression
- Multiplier applied automatically to PoA rewards at settlement
- Dashboard shows live score, tier badge, and progress to next tier

---

## Phase 3 — Real Satellite Integration
- Expand beyond LoRa simulation to receive real CubeSat transmissions
- TinyGS-compatible receiver firmware for actual low-orbit satellites
- Support NOAA, ISS SSTV, and amateur CubeSat frequencies
- Verified passes from real satellites anchored on Hedera

---

## Phase 4 — Network Expansion
- Multi-frequency support — 433 MHz, 868 MHz, 915 MHz
- Mobile ground station nodes — Raspberry Pi + LoRa HAT kits
- Global coverage map showing active stations and pass history
- Operator leaderboard by credit score and total receptions

---

## Phase 5 — Data Marketplace
- Satellite data marketplace — operators list passes for sale
- Buyers pay AZM to access raw packet data or merged images
- Revenue split between contributing stations based on credit score
- API access for businesses integrating satellite data into products

---

## Phase 6 — Hardware Financing
- Operators use on-chain credit history to access hardware loans
- High-score operators qualify for subsidised antenna and SDR kits
- Credit score becomes the foundation for real-world DePIN financing
- Partnership with hardware manufacturers for verified node kits
